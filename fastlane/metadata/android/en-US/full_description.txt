Convertly is a fast and minimal unit converter built with simplicity and privacy in mind. It supports a wide range of categories including length, weight, temperature, volume, speed, and more — all wrapped in a clean, distraction-free interface.

Designed to be lightweight, responsive, and entirely offline, Convertly gets out of your way and lets you convert units quickly, without ads, trackers, or unnecessary clutter. Whether you're a student, traveler, or just someone who hates bloated apps, Convertly is made for you.

Key Features:
• Clean, ad-free interface
• Real-time conversion updates
• Supports metric and imperial units
• 100% offline — no data collected
• Lightweight and responsive design

Privacy Policy:
Convertly does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. All conversions happen locally on your device. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising libraries included. Your usage stays completely private.
